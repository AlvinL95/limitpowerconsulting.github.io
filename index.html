What We Do

Limit Power Consulting provides advisory-only electrical feasibility and capacity assessments. We help owners and project teams understand constraints, risks, and information gaps early—before full design and capital commitments.

How We Help

  • Review of existing electrical documentation
  • Concept-level load and capacity analysis
  • Identification of constraints and assumptions
  • Feasibility and decision-support memoranda
  • Guidance on next steps prior to design

What We Don’t Do

We do not provide electrical design services, stamped drawings, construction administration, or field inspections.

When We’re Used

Typically engaged early—when teams need clarity on electrical feasibility to inform go/no-go decisions, scope development, or whether to proceed with design.
