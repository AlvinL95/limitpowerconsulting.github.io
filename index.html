WHAT WE DO

Limit Power Consulting provides advisory-only electrical feasibility and capacity assessments. We help owners, architects, and project managers understand electrical constraints, risks, and information gaps early—before design scope and capital are committed.

HOW WE HELP

  • Review of existing electrical documentation and utility information
  • Concept-level load and capacity analysis
  • Identification of constraints, assumptions, and risks
  • Feasibility and decision-support memoranda
  • Guidance on next steps prior to design

WHAT WE DON’T DO

Limit Power Consulting does not provide electrical design services, stamped drawings, construction administration, or field inspections.

WHEN WE’RE USED

Typically engaged early—when teams need clarity on electrical feasibility to inform go/no-go decisions, scope development, or whether to proceed with design.
